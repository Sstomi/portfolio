My Idea of Building Website

Simple & Modern

Djikstra said "Simplicity is prerequisite for reliability". I would add that simplicity is also powerful. I'm taking my inspiration from my experience in 書道 (calligrapy): minimalist but convey a lot of meanings and emotions.
Hence, as a Web designer, I consider that my mission is to use the last technologies and best designs to connect businesses and people, specially internationally, in the simplest and most powerful way.

Mobile friendly & Worldwide

In 2018, 52.2 percent of all website traffic worldwide was generated through mobile phones*. It means that today, providing the best experience for mobile users is fundamental. Moreover, the international strategy for the Japanese companies is relying on their digital contents. Thereby, I'm committed to provide to my partners the opportunity to reinforce their digital marketing and communication strategy by connecting with the entire world on any device.
